For Surabhi

I’m not here to change your mind.
I just wanted to be honest.

Something I need to acknowledge

I didn’t show up the way I should have.
I missed understanding how you were feeling, and that caused hurt.
Even if it wasn’t intentional, I know that doesn’t make it easier.

Something that remains true

You mattered to me before everything became difficult.
You still matter to me now.
And that stays true even if distance feels necessary.

I’m sorry — gently and sincerely —
for hurting someone I care about and respect.

There’s no expectation from this page.
No reply is needed.

If one day you feel like talking, I’ll listen.
And if not, I still wish you calm, happiness, and good days ahead.

Some connections stay close.
Some quietly teach us how to be better humans.
